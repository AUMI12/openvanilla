The Welcome Pane

This pane prompts you for what you will be packaging, what company it is for, and what operating systems you need to target. This information will be used to create default settings in later panes.

The title and organization will be used to create bundle identifiers in the form of com.organization.title.component.

The target operating system will affect whether the assistant creates a metapackage or a distribution when packaging multiple items. To target 10.3 or 10.2, a metapackage must be created.
